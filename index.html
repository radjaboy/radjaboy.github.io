What is the definition of “Home Watch?” According to the National Home Watch
Association (of which we are a member) it is “a visual inspection of a home or
property, looking for obvious issues.”


What exactly does OUR Home Watch company do?

  • Conduct detailed interior/exterior property inspections with care, checking each
    room, floor by floor
  • Promptly respond to situations we encounter - whether it be storm or pest damage, a leaky water pipe or a
    malfunctioning heating system
  • Email a detailed report after each visit using our home watch software. Reports include a date and time
    stamp, comments, thermostat readings and photographs if needed
