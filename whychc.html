Why Should You Choose Us?


Your Cape house is special. A place where long-lasting memories are made. A place for reflective quiet time or
fun-filled summer days with family and friends. So it is important to make plans for your home to be cared for
while you're away.


TRUST CAPE HOUSE CARETAKERS TO BE THAT PLAN!


Mike Walther & Laura Eldridge are licensed, bonded and insured home watch professionals. Together, we
draw from a wide range of client-focused skills and experiences. Our commitment to outstanding customer
service, watch and our tech-savvy business practices ensure that we communicate with our clients in a timely and
professional manner. Our community connections and access to resources are valuable assets. If you do not have
your own companies to call upon when service or maintenance is needed, we partner with skilled local tradesmen
who can provide services for your home and property.
